Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know the items are authentic?

Every seller is physically verified at Gikomba Market. We check their stall, ID, and business registration. Items are photographed by sellers and reviewed by our team. If you receive a counterfeit item, we'll issue a full refund.

What if the item doesn't fit?

Since these are one-of-a-kind thrift pieces, we recommend confirming measurements with the seller via WhatsApp before purchase. Some sellers offer exchanges for size issues within 24 hours if the item is unworn.

How do I pay?

Most sellers accept MPESA mobile money. Some offer Cash on Delivery within Nairobi. Never pay before confirming item availability. Always get confirmation from the seller that the item is still in stock.

How long does delivery take?

Nairobi: Same day or next day delivery.
Other counties: 1-3 days via courier.
Exact timing depends on the seller's location and your delivery address. Discuss delivery options directly with the seller.

How much does it cost to sell?

Zero setup cost. No commission for your first month. After that, we charge a small 5% commission per sale to cover platform costs, payment processing, and marketing.

What if I get scammed?

Contact us immediately at disputes@gikomba.shop with screenshots of your conversation. We investigate all claims within 24 hours and ban fraudulent users. We also work with local authorities for serious cases. Our buyer protection policy covers you.
